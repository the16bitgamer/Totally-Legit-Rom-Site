DuckTales (NES)

DuckTales for the NES (Nintendo Entertainment System) was released in 1989. Produced by Capcom, licened from Disney and licensed by Nintendo. It's a typical Capcom platofmer from the time, with a stage select and similar graphics to Mega Man. With Screwge you can Pogo jump and golf swing object to attack enemies. Overall a perfect NES game 5/7.
